What you can do here

For students

Enroll in courses, pay with UPI, watch lectures and join live sessions, download notes, and track payments.

For teachers

Publish courses, upload lectures with video or meeting links, share materials, and build quizzes.

For admins

Manage accounts, oversee the catalog, verify payments, and respond to support messages.

Sarvam Professional Education connects a Spring Boot backend with MySQL so your data stays consistent—users, courses, enrollments, payments, and messages all live in one system.

Use Login if you already have an account, or Sign Up to register as a student, teacher, or admin (as allowed by your institution).
